Sorting Visualizer

Sorting algorithms can be tricky to wrap your head around—numbers moving, comparisons happening, swaps being made—it all gets confusing fast! That’s where our Sorting Visualizer comes in.

Instead of just reading about how sorting works, why not see it in action? Just enter your numbers, pick a sorting algorithm, and watch as the bars move, swap, and organize themselves right before your eyes. Every step is visualized, making it easier (and way more fun) to understand.

Whether you're a student trying to learn sorting, a developer brushing up on algorithms, or just someone who enjoys watching things fall into place—this tool is for you!
